UnLocal is a non-profit law firm that provides free legal representation and education to undocumented immigrants in New York City. Through community-led and culturally responsive rights and policy workshops, UnLocal's aim is to identify and serve those who are most in need of caring and high quality legal representation, including minors, asylum seekers, and their families. With this collaborative model, the goal is to empower immigrant communities to respond effectively to the rapidly shifting U.S. immigration system.

The goal of this auction is to raise enough funds for UnLocal to hire another full-time immigration attorney.
